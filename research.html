Research

Traffic Routing and Congestion Optimisation

This project investigates whether adaptive congestion-aware routing can improve traffic distribution across urban transportation networks.

Methods included graph optimisation, shortest-path routing, dynamic edge weighting and supervised machine learning.

Key Findings

  • A* achieved modest efficiency improvements over Dijkstra.
  • Road topology was the dominant factor influencing congestion.
  • Adaptive rerouting improved traffic distribution.
  • Neural networks learned congestion patterns under moderate traffic loads.
